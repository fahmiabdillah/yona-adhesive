About Us

Established in July 2005, PT. Trimitra Sejati Jaya starting as a Water Based Glue Manufarturer with PT. Bersama Lemindo Abadi as a partner. To address the demands of various industries, our product YONA BOND, YONA PRESS as trade mark. Due to continuous satisfaction and incredible support from our customers, we have succeeded in developing along with technology so that we can produce new products YONA MELT, and INDOPRESS.
